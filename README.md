🧈 Dairy Products Sales Dashboard – Tableau
An interactive sales analytics dashboard built in Tableau for a dairy products business, visualizing revenue, stock levels, regional performance, and sales channel breakdown.
�
This Tableau dashboard provides a comprehensive view of dairy product sales performance across multiple dimensions — product, region, sales channel, and time — enabling data-driven business decisions.

📌 Dashboard Sections
Top 10 Products — Bar chart ranking products by total revenue (INR)
Price vs Current Stock — Scatter plot showing the relationship between unit price and stock levels
Monthly Revenue Trend — Line chart tracking revenue across all 12 months
Current Stock by Location — Bar chart comparing stock across cities (Delhi, Chandigarh, Uttar Pradesh, etc.)
Sales Channels — Revenue split across Retail (₹20.86M), Wholesale (₹20.21M), and Online (₹17.66M)
Revenue by Region — Bar chart for states including Chandigarh, Delhi, UP, Maharashtra, Kerala, Tamil Nadu, and Madhya Pradesh
Units Sold by Product — Bar chart comparing unit sales across all product categories
🛒 Products Covered
Butter · Buttermilk · Cheese · Curd · Ghee · Ice Cream · Lassi · Milk · Paneer · Yogurt
🗂️ Filters Available
Location — Filter by city/region
Product Name — Filter by one or multiple products
Sales Channel — Filter by Retail, Wholesale, or Online
🛠️ Tools Used
Tableau Desktop — Dashboard design and visualisation
Microsoft Excel / CSV — Data source

🚀 How to Use
Clone or download this repository
Open Dairy_Dashboard.twbx in Tableau Desktop
Use the filters (Location, Product Name, Sales Channel) to explore the data interactively
